Help Out

Millions of people around the world suffer with anxiety disorders. While you might not be able to help everyone, educating and helping the people around you would make a big difference. Here are some ways to spread awareness and support about anxiety in your community:

  • Look for signs: Your family, friends, or other people around you may be suffering with anxiety. Make sure to look out for signs and symptoms so you could be there to help.

  • Educate yourself: Know how to support people with anxiety. You can help by simply talking to them and letting them know you are there. Also, research places in your area where professional help can be received.

  • Educate others: Let others know about the signs and symptoms of anxiety so they could help as well. You could also spread the word about the seriousness of anxiety by talking with others or through social media.
